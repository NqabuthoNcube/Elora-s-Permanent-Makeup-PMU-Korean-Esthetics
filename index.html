Our Process

A Safe, Comfortable
Experience

Every step is carefully designed to ensure natural results, maximum comfort, and complete safety — from consultation to final touch-up.

01

Personalised Consultation

We assess your lip tone, shape, and facial features to design a colour and style that enhances your natural beauty. Everything is mapped and approved before we begin.

02

Comfort Preparation

A medical-grade numbing cream is applied to ensure a comfortable experience. Most clients feel minimal to no discomfort throughout the session.

03

Precision Lip Blushing

Using sterile, professional PMU equipment, pigment is gently deposited to create a soft, even tint. The session typically takes 90–120 minutes.

04

Healing & Perfecting Touch-Up

Aftercare guidance is provided to support proper healing. A touch-up session at 6–8 weeks ensures colour balance and long-lasting results.
